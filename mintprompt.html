About Mintprompt

Mintprompt is a curated marketplace connecting AI prompt creators with buyers seeking high-quality workflows, automation templates, and creative prompts. The platform enables creators to monetize their expertise while providing users access to battle-tested prompts that work across multiple AI platforms including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and custom AI implementations.

Built with a focus on quality over quantity, every prompt on Mintprompt undergoes manual review to ensure reliability and value. The platform operates on a creator-first modelâ€”sellers keep 100% of their revenue, with Auric earning through subscription fees rather than commission.

Purpose & Technology

The platform addresses a critical gap in the AI ecosystem: the lack of a trusted marketplace for discovering and acquiring proven prompts. As AI adoption accelerates, the need for reliable, well-documented workflows grows exponentially.

Mintprompt leverages modern web technologies including Next.js for performance, PostgreSQL for data integrity, and Stripe for secure payment processing. The architecture prioritizes speed, security, and scalability to support growing demand.

  • Manual quality review for all submissions
  • Creator-friendly revenue model (100% to sellers)
  • Multi-platform prompt compatibility
  • SEO-optimized product pages
  • Secure payment infrastructure
  • Automated digital delivery

Future Roadmap

Development priorities include expanding the prompt library across new categories, introducing advanced search and filtering capabilities, implementing user reviews and ratings, and building AI-powered prompt testing tools to validate quality before publication.

Long-term plans involve creating a collaborative environment where creators can iterate on existing prompts, fork workflows, and contribute to a shared knowledge base that accelerates AI adoption across industries.
